FeelFlow AI: Speech Emotion Recognition

Mental health is a trending hot-topic that is gaining traction in its importance. The group most faced with the mental struggles are the youths, with issues stemming from work, school, family, and/or relationships.

With that, I developed a prediction tool based on a modified Google WaveNet model for Speech Emotion Recognition to analyse audio characteristics. This alleviates the stress on mental health professionals to assess patients' emotional well-being, aiding in accurate diagnostics and treatment plans.

Tools-Libraries used: Pandas | NumPy | Matplotlib | Seaborn | Scikit-Learn | yt-dlp (YouTube AV scraper) | PyDub | Spleeter | Librosa | PyCaret | Pipeline | RandomSearchCV | Tensorflow | Keras | WaveNet | LSTM | Streamlit

In A Heartbeat: Early Detection of Heart Disease Risk

Being the leading cause of death in the US, our comprehensive study utilises advanced machine learning models and neural networks to predict heart disease risk by thoroughly analysing possible risk factors - demographic data, lifestyle habits, and medical history. The project aims to enable early detection, and thereby intervention to improve health outcomes.

Tools-Libraries used: Pandas | NumPy | Matplotlib | Seaborn | Scikit-Learn | LazyPredict (Baseline Model) | Pipeline | GridSearchCV | SMOTE | ADASyn | Logistic Regression | XGBoost | Random Forest | Decision Trees | Tensorflow | Keras | Streamlit

Scam or Not?

Scams are increasingly prevalent, with Singapore reporting a record high of over 46,000 cases in 2023, leading to significant financial losses. This project develops a classifier using Natural Language Processing to assist Reddit moderators in distinguishing between scams and genuine acts of kindness on the platform. The model leverages TF-IDF vectorisation and Multinomial Naive-Bayes to categorise Reddit posts, aiming to enhance online safety through moderation efficiency.

Tools-Libraries used: Pandas | NumPy | Matplotlib | Seaborn | Wordcloud | Scikit-Learn | praw (Reddit webscraping API) | RegEx | NLTK | LazyPredict (Baseline Model) | Pipeline | GridSearchCV | Word2Vec | CountVectorizer | TF-IDF | Logistic Regression | Naive-Bayes | Streamlit

Resale HDB Price Prediction

This project aims to develop a machine learning model to predict HDB resale prices in Singapore. By utilising features such as HDB characteristics, surrounding amenities, transportation convenience, and proximity to top secondary schools, the model leverages linear regression to provide accurate pricing forecasts, aiding buyers and sellers in informed decision-making.

Tools-Libraries used: Pandas | NumPy | Matplotlib | Seaborn | Scikit-Learn | Linear Regression | Regularisation (Ridge & Lasso) | Streamlit
